🏡 Welcome to LandInfoTG

LandInfoTG is a reliable real estate information platform built to simplify land verification and property research. It provides accurate land records, legal status checking, and ownership details in one place. Our goal is to make property transactions safer, faster, and transparent for everyone.

📜 EC Page

The EC (Encumbrance Certificate) page allows users to verify if a property has any loans or liabilities.
It shows registered transactions related to the property.
Users can confirm legal clarity before buying or selling.
It ensures safe and secure property dealings.
This page reduces financial and legal risks significantly.

📖 ROR Page

The ROR (Record of Rights) page provides complete ownership details.
It displays land survey number and classification.
Users can check land history and mutation records.
It confirms rightful property holders.
This ensures trust and transparency in transactions.

🚫 Prohibited Page

This page helps users check restricted properties.
It identifies government-blocked lands.
Prevents illegal or disputed purchases.
Protects buyers from legal complications.
Ensures compliance with regulations.

🔍 Land Search Page

Search land records using survey number or district.
Quick access to property details.
Simple and user-friendly interface.
Saves time during property verification.
Improves efficiency in land research.

📂 Documents Page

Access essential land-related documents.
Download required certificates easily.
Supports verification and registration process.
Keeps records organized in one place.
Makes documentation simple and fast.
